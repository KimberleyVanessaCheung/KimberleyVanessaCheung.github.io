01. About Me

Hello! I'm Kimberley, a creative technologist and professional fencing athlete exploring the intersection of human performance, augmentation, and technology.

With a BSc in Biochemistry from HKUST and an MSc in Computing from Imperial College London, I bridge the gap between biological sciences and cutting-edge technology. My unique background allows me to approach human performance optimization from both a scientific and technological perspective.

As a professional fencing athlete with many years of competitive experience, I'm deeply invested in understanding how technology can enhance human capabilities. My work focuses on developing innovative applications that combine machine learning, computer vision, and biometric data to push the boundaries of human potential.

Currently exploring projects at the intersection of ArtTech and human augmentation—creating interactive experiences that not only engage but actively enhance human performance and well-being. My athletic background provides unique insights into the practical applications of performance technology.
